Current Features

At a glance

Look out for the upcoming features above.

Users

Once a user has logged in securely via Steam, he/she is granted full access to the platform. The user can now browse other users and teams, join multiple existing teams or create a new team to be able to book scrims.

Teams

When creating a team, the team leader sets a number of options like team skill level, time zone and availability in week days. This makes finding other teams with matching characteristics really easy.

Scrims

A team leader can propose a scrim to another team using a simple form. The time are locked to the specified availability dates and are automatically converted into the different teams' time zones. The first map is proposed by the challenging team and the second map is choses when the second team accepts the challenge
